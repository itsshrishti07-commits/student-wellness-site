Stress Management Tips

🧘 Practice Mindfulness

Spend 5 minutes every day focusing on your breath. It lowers cortisol levels and improves focus.

🏃 Physical Activity

Exercise releases endorphins. Even a 10-minute walk can significantly boost your mood.

🍎 Eat Healthy

Avoid excessive caffeine and sugar. High-protein snacks help keep your energy stable during study sessions.

Quote of the Day

"It's not the load that breaks you down, it's the way you carry it."
