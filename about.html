About the Project

The Student Wellness & Stress Management System is a project designed to tackle the rising mental health concerns among college students. By providing accessible tools, we aim to encourage early self-reflection.


Key Features:

  • Smart Stress Detection
  • Encrypted-like Local Storage Diary
  • Motivational Resources
